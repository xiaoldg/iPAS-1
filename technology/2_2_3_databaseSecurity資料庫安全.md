```
Identifying Threats:
The threats that are revealed by the penetration test can then be categorized using STRIDE methodology . 
 
 STRIDE stands for
• Spoofing identity
• Tampering with data
• Repudiation
• Information disclosure資訊外洩
• Denial of service (DoS)阻斷攻擊
• Elevation of privileges 
```
